Our Current Undertakings

Our primary goal is to develop a comprehensive ecosystem in which children from various grades and mixed backdrop can learn, think critically and creatively, extend their view beyond accepted norms and grow unhindered.

All the current undertakings aim at providing each child, regardless of their socio-economic heritage, a uniform scope, a space to learn outside the centralised curricula and explore "learning" in an individualistic manner.

Shiksha Sanjeevanee is the trade-mark project that includes one annual book distribution ceremony and year-long educational assistance for grade 6 to 9.
